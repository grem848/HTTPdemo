Nginx & SSL


What is the purpose on Nginx (a reverse proxy) as used in this week

Nginx is used to protect our internal network, it has better protection than tomcat,
and it can be used for load balancing too. Reverse proxies can be used for security,
performance, load balancing, caching and so on.
We used Nginx to protect and take requests then forward them to tomcat.
